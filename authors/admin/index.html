David Lilien

University of Copenhagen

I’m a postdoc in glaciology at the University of Copenhagen. I study the dynamics of ice streams and outlet glaciers using numerical models, remote sensing, and field data. My primary interest is understanding recent changes to glaciers in West Antarctica and Greenland, and understanding the context of those changes through analysis of past ice flow.
